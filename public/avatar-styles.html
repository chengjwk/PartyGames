Dragon — 4 styles to compare

Same character, four visual flavors. Pick one and the other 23 avatars get redrawn in that style.

A. Friendly Flat (current)

Mid-detail flat colors, big simple eyes, no outlines. What the game ships with today.

B. Kawaii Chibi

Oversized head, huge sparkly eyes, tiny features, pastel palette, lots of blush. Very young-kid friendly.

C. Outlined Sticker

Thick black outlines around saturated fills. Pokémon / Splatoon / sticker pack feel. Very legible at small sizes.

D. Storybook Illustrated

More layered shading, drawn scales, asymmetric details. Children's-book illustration. Heavier visual weight.
